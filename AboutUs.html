Wedding Wish is a fairly new organization, with the primary objective of providing wedding information for brides of every variety. Beginning in Ocala, Florida, the horse capital of the world, having a wedding that deviated from the rustic barn motif came across as a challenge. We at Wedding Wish embrace the idea of having the wedding the bride desires at any location, within an affordable budget.
